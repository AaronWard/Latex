Letters to the editorLetters for publication should be sent to these addresses. The sender’s full postal address and phone number are required, for verification only:Guardian: guardian.letters@theguardian.comObserver: observer.letters@observer.co.uk
Corrections and clarifications of editorial content – the readers’ editorsIt is the policy of the Guardian and the Observer to correct significant editorial errors and handle editorial complaints as soon as possible. The readers’ editors are independent internal ombudsmen and can be contacted as follows:
Paul Chadwick – Guardian global readers’ editorguardian.readers@theguardian.comGuardian readers’ editor page
observer.readers@observer.co.uk
Please note that queries about subscriptions, vouchers, reader offers, advertising, marketing and other non-editorial areas are not handled by the readers’ editors. These should be addressed to the relevant non-editorial departments. Story suggestions should also be sent to the relevant editorial departments.
Legal complaintsLegal complaints should be addressed to our Editorial Legal Services department at: Kings Place, 90 York Way London N1 9GU

To report a suspected security vulnerability 
If you spot a potential security vulnerability in the Guardian’s websites and apps, we’d be grateful to hear from you. Please send an encrypted email to appsec@theguardian.com using this PGP key. If possible, include screenshots in your description. We may contact you for more details. Thanks for your help.

Getting the Guardian abroad
The availability of the Guardian and Observer in newsagents is very limited outside of the UK. However, you can still purchase these publications in a small selection of countries and cities, or you can subscribe and have your copy delivered to your door.
For more information on purchasing copies of the Guardian abroad, please contact the international publishing team.
You can subscribe to the Guardian by clicking here and Observer abroad by clicking here or contact our customer service department at Quadrant Subscription Services:
Telephone: +44 (0)203 353 2140 Fax: +44 (0) 208 261 8433 Email: guardianpostal@theguardian.com
Address: The Guardian and Observer Quadrant Subscription Services, Rockwood House, 9-17 Perrymount Road, Hayward Heath RH16 3DH, United Kingdom
